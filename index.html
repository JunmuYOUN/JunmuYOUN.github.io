About

I am an integrated M.S.–Ph.D. student (Ph.D. candidate) at the Sun & Space Weather Lab, Kyung Hee University, supervised by Prof. Yong-Jae Moon. My research centers on deep learning applications in solar physics and space weather: determining coronal temperatures (DEM) from Solar Orbiter EUV observations, and detecting aurora in satellite time-series images with explainable AI.
